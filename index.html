My Work

Seismic Retrofit, Ashland HS, Summer 2024

I worked as a project engineer intern for Outlier General Construction in Medford, Oregon. I was assigned to the Ashland High School project, which involved a seismic retrofit of their Humanities Building (a three-story building), renovation of their science building, a new fire sprinkler and alarm system, and much more! School Renovation, Clear Lake Elementary School, Summer 2022

I worked as a laborer for Bethel School District (Eugene, Oregon) and was put on the Clear Lake Elementary School renovation project. From demolition to installing casework and the final punch walk, I was involved. This was my first construction project, and I learned a lot about how to work efficiently with deadlines. I also had the opportunity to work with a great team and contribute to creating something meaningful and long-lasting for the community. The project turned two very large classrooms into three smaller classrooms with a ADA rated bathroom and kitchen/laundry room. (See more details in the linked resume below).

About Me

My passion for building began at a young age while helping my grandfather with projects around his property. Those experiences inspired me to pursue a career in construction management. I find the construction process especially rewarding because it allows me to be involved in every stage of a project, from initial excavation to the final punch walk with the owner. I enjoy the constant opportunities to learn, solve problems, and contribute to creating something meaningful and long-lasting. In addition, my strong mathematical background has helped me develop an analytical mindset, attention to detail, and the ability to work efficiently with deadlines.
